Battery and gears
The strong battery (960 Wh, LG-cells) together with the Bafang engine (350w) make it possible to travel a distance of up to 50 miles per charge (depending on weather, your own weight, and terrain). With the five gears it can be switched to a speed you feel comfortable with.
Easy operation and very safeOperating this is so simple, anyone can use it! We have riders from 9 to 90 who enjoy their bike each and every day. Simply set the bike for the speed you are comfortable starting on, hold the 'Auto' button to engage the treadmill, step up and simply walk with the treadmill. With the exception of the treadmill component on the bike, bikes are made with customary bicycle parts.it is equipped with regular handbrake levers on the handlebars and disc brakes on the wheel assembly to ensure safe, comfortable and smooth stopping power.

Frame/Chassis
Designed specifically for use in a solar/hybrid configuration, the 100% Aircraft Grade 6061T aluminum construction set the bar of advanced recumbent design. Designed without paint eliminates toxins and stainless steel hardware lowers maintenance and corrosion. Large diameter 26” wheels smooth out the bumps, decreases rolling resistance and creates a practical ride height that inspires confidence in traffic. Dynamic Dampening Front Suspension combined with the dual-spine construction is super light, comfortable and capable of up to a 550 lb. payload. Comes with a 10 year guarantee.
Intelligent Energy System
In addition to the UPDrive, this is an advanced evolution of the original Dual Drive. Both the motor and the pedal system operate independently and harmoniously. This allows each drive line to operate with maximum efficiency and never dependent on the other. A 750W DC Permanent Neodymium Magnet Motor routed through a 48V programmable controller is custom configured to a proprietary Brain Box providing continuous solar power whether parked or in motion. Lithium-ion Manganese batteries are made in the USA with a built-in Battery Management System (BMS) and are guaranteed for 1 year. When combined with the optional NuVinci® continuously variable transmission technology, a smooth integration of muscle and electrons is achieved unlike anything previously available.
